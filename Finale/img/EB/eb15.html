Republic City Application - Wu Po by KimTheKat

Watch
Fan Art / Cartoons & Comics / Digital / Other©2012-2019 KimTheKat



Name : Wu Po

Age : 17

Nationality : Republic City

Affinity : Firebender

Skills :

{ Graffiti Art } Wu uses her artistic talents and quickness in order to plaster her ideals on the walls of Republic City. She has been practicing this way of graffiti since she was a young teen and has become quite good at it.

{ Knowledge of the City } Wu may not know how to use a compass but she sure does know her way around town. Though many years she has learnt the best hiding spots, the best graffiti spots, and all the quick back routes useful to evade any followers.

{ Free Running } Every good criminal needs the ability to escape the police. Wu's way is to free run across the tops of buildings, through tight alleyways, and up walls. She is fairly good at it, but only because of her familiarity with the city. In new situations she may be slightly clumsy, but she will adapt to the new terrain.


Personality:

{ Short Attention Span } Wu has always needed action and interest in her life. She loves new challenges and heightened adrenaline. Unfortunately his has caused a very short attention span. She often gets bored with things like school or studying. Therefore, she has a lowered education. But this doesn't mean that she's stupid, using many experiences in the past she has street smarts.

{ Brash } Wu isn't going to beat around the bush. She is going to tell you how it is in any situation. This sometimes comes across has more uncaring than truthful though and it may also seem like she is putting others down in hope to raise her status. But none of these are true. She honestly feels like it will end up benefiting the person in the future if they realize their mistakes now.

{ High Self Confidence } Although Wu isn't very good at it, she loves a good fight. She loves the regular fist fighting and often goes to illegal rings in order to watch and bet on the fighters. She attempts to fight herself, but usually ends up being beaten herself. It has never lead to a falter in her self-confidence though because she keeps a disposition that she was just having a 'bad day'.

Other traits: Confident | Intrusive | Rude | Impulsive | Hot-headed

RP sample:
It was another cool morning in Republic City as thick fog from the bay moved in. Slowly people began to filter the streets as Wu watched from above. They headed to restaurants, jobs, and other responsibilities. She smirked, amused by their boring lifestyles that were so repetitive. Every morning they do the same thing at the same times. She looked back on the exciting days that she had, and laughed, not caring who heard.

Additional Info:
-Wu is a hardcore Equalist. Most of her graffiti is centered around the nonbender movements or around how she thinks the government is faulty.
-She hates plans. They are not useful and time consuming to her. She would rather think quick on her feet and go with the punches.
-When Wu in graffiti painting she wears a fox mask to hide her identity and to develop a name for herself.
- Wu does actually have a job, but it is more of a random working job of delivering goods to people around the city. She does it periodically in order to gain money, or when the people really need her.

Choices:
1. Non Bender
2. Fire
3. Earth
