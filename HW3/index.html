Knight
"For where there is adventure there is also a knight."

A knights fighting style is as important as the strength of their arms or the sharpness of their blade. Kights fight by using their skills, strategy, and tactics. Typically a knight will use a broadsword; however, its not uncommon for a knight to use other weapons like a warhammer, mace, or greatsword. Adventure and glory awaits us!
Mage
"A mages' soul is forged in the nature of magic."

Mages are a superb magic fighters; they spend many years honing their craft. With the mistic arts on your side, reality can be whatever you make it: change staw into gold, heal uncurable wounds, cause massive earthquakes, or even re-animate the dead. Mages use staffs or relics to help focus magical engergy. Be wary of dangerous magical items.
Rogue
"Every town and city has its share of rogues."

Rogues are elite sleath fighters that use the cover of night to carry out their plans. A skillful enough rogues can steal armor right of a guards back. Speed is a rouge friend; not being fast enough can result in a failed heist. Typically rouges fight with nimble weapons like daggers, rapiers, or short-bows. Rogues are not trained to fight fair; Rogues are trained to win.
